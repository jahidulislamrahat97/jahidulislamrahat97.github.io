Cleaner X

Cleaner X is a project which is used to reduce trash from the water, collect trash from the local area & creating awareness among people about not throwing waste, using an automatic trash collector boat & apps. In 2019 I got a Runner-up in the NASA Space Apps challenge 2019 using this project.

Mainly in this project, we are made 2 solutions,1st is removing waste from the sea location where itâ€™s already polluted, and 2nd is to create awareness among people about not throwing waste into waters and the bad impact of this. To Fulfill the 1st solutions we build an automatic trash collector boat that will collect floating and submerged waste, and dirt from the water and store it in the storage section. So, It can collect a large amount of floating water from the river & sea. After collecting the waste it will then dump all the waste into the dumping station. From there it will be sorted and will be sent for Recycle.

For raising awareness, we build an app that will act as a social network. For dumping every bag of waste we will give the user a point. Also, there will be a feature where a user can upload & check-in a picture of the waste pile. Then Related corporations can find out the waste pile easily and can remove it for disposal. For that contribution, the user will get 5 points. Every Year we will select the highest point holder nationally and internationally and recognize him as a "Green Man Of The Earth".
