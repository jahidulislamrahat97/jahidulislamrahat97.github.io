RA-Bot (Research Assistant Robot)

RA-Bot is a Research Assistant Robot. IT is a 5 DOF Robotic arm that is controlled by desktop software by remotely. It can be controlled repeatedly & live control also. It is used for helping researchers to work with chemical hazards, explosive materials, viruses, bacteria, etc. the main purpose of this robot is to keep safe a researcher from any kind of disaster. In 2018 I got this idea & implemented it & got a Research grant from Bangladesh ICT Division in 2019, Unfortunately for COVID 19 Bangladesh govt stop funding for research then the project was closed.
