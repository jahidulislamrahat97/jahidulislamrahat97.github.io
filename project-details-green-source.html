Green Source

Green Source is a project which is used to reduce deforestation using UAVs. In Bangladesh, I introduce this project for the 1st time. In 2019 I got a championship in the NASA Space Apps challenge 2019 using this project & I got a Research grant from Bangladesh ICT Division. Then in 2020 Bangladesh Air Force uses these ideas & implements them on large scale.

Mainly in this project, we use different types of UAVs like Quad Copter, Hexa Copter, and Octa copter & also design an extension for dropping seed balls (Seed balls, also known as earth balls, consist of a variety of different seeds rolled within a ball of clay, preferably volcanic pyroclastic red clay. Various additives may be included, such as hummus or compost.). It has fully autonomous & semi-autonomous, manual control section also.
