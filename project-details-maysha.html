Maysha

Maysha is a service bot, especially it is a restaurant service bot for serving food but it is also used for anchoring service/& information desk robot. it has fully autonomous and manual control. Indoor it uses track following image processing for its movement & at the outdoor we control it manually for movement. it can communicate in Bangla & English language. it was used in the grand opening of the 5G ceremony.

I work on this project as an Internship trainee, here I work in the electrical & mechanical driving section. I also do 2d design of its base plate & programming it for anchoring services.
